Top Videogames of All Time According to Odd

Three of my favorite games.

Psychonauts

Incredibly funny, great writing, and a unique style that holds up today.

Elden Ring

Felt like I was a kid again, gave me that wonder of exploration that many games try to emulate but struggle to properly do.

Hollow Knight

I couldn't put it down except to sleep, a beautiful world filled with all kinds of mysteries and incredibly simple, yet challenging combat.
